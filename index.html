    What I do.

    Deep Learning Research

    My research interests lie in enhancing the cognitive capabilities of deep neural networks to accelerate technological and humanitarian progress, doing so via mimicking the human learning process through intelligently manipulating and distilling the optimization landscape.

    Machine Learning Specialist @Actionable

    Improving the quality of employee training programs with deep learning. Built + deployed cross-architecture GAN/MLP/XGBoost Recommendation Engine via Flask, Linux, and AWS. Keyword extraction w/LSTMs and Cosine similarity.

    Host Youth STEM Workshops at Innovire

    I volunteer at Innovire, an organization helping mentor underprivileged youth regarding STEM. I've hosted workshops, talks, and hackathons on computing and artificial intelligence - most recently at the University of Toronto Mississauga.

    The Knowledge Society (TKS) Alumni

    A human accelerator with fantastic mentors and a brilliant community. Worked with the US National Committee for UN Women on digital literacy solutions and proposed a winning concept for thermoelectric nuclear reactors as PM for both teams.

    Host the 3 Bucket Podcast

    A podcast based on 3 pillars - technology, productivity, and philosophy. The goal is having deep, insightful, and thought-provoking conversations with inspiring young people.

    This project is currently on hiatus to pursue research interests.

    Found Companies To Solve Problems

    I've worked on a lot of "failed" ventures, each of which 10x-ed my engineering, designing, and problem-solving abilities. Most notable is Crysta, an all-in-one productivity platform for students based on non-invasive energy level tracking to find optimal personal work routines.
